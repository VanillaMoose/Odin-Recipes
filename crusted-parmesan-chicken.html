Crusted Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. Chicken Breasts or Tenders
  • ~2 C. Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 2 C. Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 C. Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 Stick Unsalted Butter
  • 2 cloves Minced Garlic
  • ~1/2 lb. Spaghetti Noodles
  • 2 C. Marinara Sauce (or regular tomato sauce)
  • 2 C. Mozzarella Cheese
    • SEASONINGS (1 T each): thyme, basil, oregano, paprika, onion powder, salt, pepper

Directions:

  1. Oil a 9x13 baking dish and set your oven to 350 degrees F for the chicken. Then begin boiling water in a pot for your pasta.
  2. Pat the chicken dry and set aside to rest while you prepare the butter garlic mixture and breading.
  3. In a medium sized bowl combine the dijon mustard, butter (softened), and garlic. Mix well. In another medium sized bowl combine the seasonings, parmesan cheese, and breadcrumbs. Mix well.
  4. Dip each chicken breast in the butter mixture, being sure each side is fully coated, and then lay them next to each other in the oiled 9x13 pan. Sprinkle a thick layer of the seasoned breadcrumb mixture over the chicken, gently pushing the breadcrumbs down to ensure as much as possible sticks to the chicken.
  5. Bake for 40-50 minutes, or until the breading is golden brown and there is no pink in the center of the chicken.
  6. While still in the baking dish pour the marinara sauce (or tomato sauce) over the chicken and then sprinkle the mozzarella over top of the layer of sauce. Bake for another 15-20 minutes or until the cheese starts to turn golden.

Serve and Enjoy!
