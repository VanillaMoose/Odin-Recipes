Avocado Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 Ripe Avocados
  • 1 C. Heavy Cream
  • 1 C. Whole Milk (or coconut milk)
  • 3/4 C. Sweetener of choice (sugar, brown sugar, honey, etc.)
  • 2 tsp Lemon Juice
OPTIONAL: mint, walnut, or other additional ingredients for added flavor

Directions:

  1. Scoop out the meat of the avocados and add to a blender.
  2. Add in the heavy cream, milk, sweetener, and lemon juice and blend until well combined and smooth.
  3. Dump into ice cream machine immediately or let chill in the fridge for 3+ hours first.
  4. Churn for 10-15 minutes and then transfer to a container to sit in the freezer for 2-3 hours or until consistently frozen.
  5. Garnish with toffee crumbles, mint leaves, or any other topping of choice.

Serve and Enjoy!
